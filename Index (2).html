Frequently Asked Questions

What is GEN AI?

Gen AI goes beyond AI and creates original content in text, images, video, audio or software code—in response to a user’s prompt or request. Gen AI tools such as ChatGPT, Dall-E, GitHub, Gemini etc., have made worldwide headlines and launched an unprecedented surge of innovation and adoption in all spheres including marketing.

What are the applications of Gen AI in marketing?

Gen AI is redefining marketing – from improving content efficiency to enhancing personalization of campaigns. Research shows leading organisations across industries such as BFSI, eCommerce, Retail, FMCG, Automobiles and IT are adopting Gen AI for optimizing ad copy, visualization, streamlining data analysis and generating consumer insights.
